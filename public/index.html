about

Hi! I'm Stella. I'm from and based in Los Angeles.

I work at Kaya Press, an independent publisher of APIDA-diaspora writers.

I studied English Literature and Urban Sustainable Planning at the University of Southern California.

I'm interested in the ways we can engage urban planning with community organizing, arts & culture development, and thoughtful architecture.
